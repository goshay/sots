Sounds of the South

The sound of the south has taken over Hip-hop. A record from any part of the world has the dynamics that make it sound like something straight from the south. Even with southern hip-hop showing up a little in every record, the artist that curate that sound are often forgotten or discredited. Meet some of the southern artists that are keeping hip-hop afloat.
